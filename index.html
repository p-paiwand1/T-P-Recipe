Tomato Pizza Recipe

Learn How to make a Tomato Pizza with all its instructions and the ingredients that are in need while preparing so.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon yellow cornmeal
  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasteurized Mozzarella
  • Tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper, Black Pepper

Instructions For Pizza Dough

  1. 1/2 cup warm water ( 105 to 110 degrees F).
  2. 2 teaspoons dry yeast.
  3. 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  4. 1 teaspoon Salt.
  5. 3 tablespoons Olive Oil.
